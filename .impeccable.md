## Design Context

### Users
General consumers using a telecom self-service mobile app. They check balance, monitor usage, purchase bundles, and get support. Primary context is mobile PWA — quick interactions while on-the-go. Users are not technical and need clarity over cleverness.

### Brand Personality
**Modern & Bold** — Like a tech startup that knows what it's doing. Confident, distinctive, not afraid to stand out. But warmth matters — these are people's personal accounts, not corporate systems.

### Aesthetic Direction
Inspired by **Notion** — warm minimal, soft edges, calm palette with moments of delight. But applied through a telecom lens: clean information density, clear hierarchy, micro-interactions that feel considered rather than flashy.

### Design Principles
1. **Warmth over cold tech** — Soft corners, gentle shadows, approachable typography. Not sterile, not robotic.
2. **Clarity at a glance** — Users should understand their account status in under 3 seconds. Information hierarchy is paramount.
3. **Confident simplicity** — Fewer elements, but each one is intentional and refined. No decoration for decoration's sake.
4. **Delight through precision** — Subtle animations, refined micro-interactions. The quality is in the details.
5. **Trust through transparency** — Clear states (loading, error, success), no hidden complexity, honest UI.

### Theme
Light mode (primary, warm and approachable) + Dark mode (sophisticated, easy on eyes). User toggle in corner. Not purple-everything — use a distinctive accent that feels owned.
